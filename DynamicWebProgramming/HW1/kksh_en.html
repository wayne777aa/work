KKSH

A Local High School in Kaohsiung

I attended Guoguang Laboratory School, National Sun Yat-sen University (KKSH) for both junior and senior high. Although it's not ranked among the top schools, the school culture is simple and the learning atmosphere is excellent. Overall, it's a good school. I eventually got into NYCU through The Star Plan, mainly because it didn't require an interview.

KKSH's Specialty: Oasis

When I first entered junior high, the vending machines only sold one drink—“Oasis”—besides water. My family told me that in the past there were also sports drinks like FIN, but I never knew why they were removed.

By the time I was in my third year of senior high, a 7-11 vending machine was introduced, which saved us poor students—though I thought it was still quite overpriced.
