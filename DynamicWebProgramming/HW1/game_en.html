Games

Most Thrilling Game: Sekiro

Parrying—commonly called "clashing swords"—creates crisp, rhythmic sound effects. Once you become familiar with the boss patterns, you can enjoy smooth, intense battles. For me, it's stress-relieving; sometimes when I'm tired from homework, I'll jump into Sekiro just to parry a few fights.

My Favorite 2D Metroidvania: Hollow Knight

The combat feels great, bosses don't have too many moves but are still challenging, and the exploration offers a strong sense of progression. I've been waiting forever for the sequel, Silksong, finally, no longer a clown. I played over 40 hours in just one weekend, which shows how much I love it.
